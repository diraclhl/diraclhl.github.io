ABOUT

Hi! Welcome to my website.

I completed my Ph.D thesis in theoretical nuclear physics at the GANIL laboratory and the University of Caen Normandy. Afterwards, I did my first postdoc at the French Institute for Radiation Protection and Nuclear Safety (IRSN).

Currently, I am working at Inria Grenoble Rhône-Alpes as a postdoctoral research fellow, attached to the Mistis team and collaborating with Florence Forbes and Julyan Arbel, with the intention of deepening my knowledge and skills in statistics and machine learning.

Outside of my work, I like so much traveling and exploring new and interesting places. Besides, I am also passionate about astrophotography.
